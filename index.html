Integrating AI and Systems-Based Approaches for Sustainability Management and Responsible Innovation

Strengthen leadership and managerial skills for responsible innovation and future-ready organizational practices. Explore how systems thinking and AI (including large language models) can support ethically aligned, sustainable decision-making and action.

Call For Papers

We are soliciting working papers and full papers that examine perspectives to anticipate industry and societal impacts while defining values, objectives, and ethical principles that support sustainable entrepreneurship and management. Papers should emphasize inclusive engagement strategies, integrating diverse stakeholder perspectives—from experts to end-users—to ensure responsiveness and sustainability. We also seek submissions that evaluate global risks, sustainability dimensions, technological implications of design and deployment, and practices to enhance leadership with integrity and resilience. Papers could also explore how systems thinking and AI (focusing on large language models) can serve as strategic partners in analyzing complex business cases and co-creating actionable recommendations. Each paper presentation will allow time for developmental feedback from participants. After the workshop, attendees are invited to join a community network, (Responsible Innovation for Sustainability Network [RISN]), to foster ongoing collaboration and knowledge exchange across sectors.

Topics of Interest

Responsible innovation Systems thinking Sustainability management AI integration LLMs for decision support Stakeholder engagement Ethics & governance Risk & resilience

Accepted contributions will be presented and discussed with developmental feedback. Program details will be announced closer to the conference date.

Why now

At a time when multiple planetary boundaries that support life on Earth have been breached, prudence and responsibility are essential amid rapid technological growth.

What you’ll gain

  • Insights on systems-based sustainability management
  • Responsible innovation strategies and governance perspectives
  • Discussion with peers and developmental feedback on your work
